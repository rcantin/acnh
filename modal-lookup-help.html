Critter Lookup - What is it?

You can search for a Critter you're interested in by typing in its name. Searches are still limited by the other filters you have set, so if you search for a Bug but your Critter Type is set to Fish, you won't see any matches.

Filter

When you use the Filter option, your results will show only the matching Critters and hide everything else.

Highlight

When you use the Highlight option, all Critters stay visible, but Critters that match your search will be highlighted.
